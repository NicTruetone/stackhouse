About Stackhouse

Stackhouse is a small steak sandwich shop in Vancouver, with one location on Davie Street and one in Gastown.

We keep the menu short. The Stackhouse steak sandwich is medium rare sirloin with crispy onion and chimichurri. There's also a beef dip, BBQ pulled pork, steak tartare, sides like French onion soup and caesar salad, and coffee if you feel like sitting a while.

Everything's made to order, so give it a couple of minutes.
