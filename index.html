Crafted with Care

Stackhouse is a small sandwich shop on Davie Street in Vancouver, dedicated to doing one thing extraordinarily well: steak sandwiches.

We use tender, medium-rare steak with fresh bread and our signature chimichurri sauce. Every ingredient is selected for quality and flavor. Every sandwich is made with precision.

It's simple. It's fresh. It's unforgettable.
